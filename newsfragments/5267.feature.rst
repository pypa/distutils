The C compilers no longer depend on ``distutils.spawn``, ``distutils.dir_util``, ``distutils.file_util``, ``distutils._modified``, or ``distutils.util.execute``/``split_quoted``. The generic build primitives (``spawn``, ``newer``/``newer_group``, ``split_quoted``) are now vendored into the ``compilers`` package, and the ``Compiler.mkpath``/``move_file``/``execute`` methods are implemented directly on the standard library (``os.makedirs``/``shutil.move``). The methods are retained for backward compatibility.
